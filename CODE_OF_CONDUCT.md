# Code of Conduct

DeepEye aims to be a respectful, technical, and collaborative project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, design, and evidence.
- Assume good intent, but be clear when something is unsafe, incorrect, or out of scope.
- Respect privacy and do not share secrets, private datasets, or confidential details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions
- Sharing exploitable security details in public channels
- Any behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or hostile

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from project spaces.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers through the repository's available private channels.
